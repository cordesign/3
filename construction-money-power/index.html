Editorial

The magazine is called “3” for its significance (leadership, strength and ambition); It was developed with the theme ‘World Anthropocene’; It focuses on three major themes: Construction, Money and Power. For each block of text in English has at his side the corresponding block of text in Portuguese different text color. Each page has about 300 words and 3 images per page; each theme will have 3 articles; there will be 3 articles sizes: small or large or small; content is arranged in a grid made up of horizontal lines with a spacing of 12pts;

The magazine has a size 45pts 66pts is left a margin of 6cm from the outside of the sheet 3 white lines starting from the margin of the top and two white rows starting margin baixo.Hierarquicamente there is article name and body text. I used the font “Bickham Script Pro ‘and’ Ebrima ‘on the cover and’ Arial ‘inside; the font in paragraphs never varies, and has a fixed size 10pts ‘Narrow’ for the body and 17pts ‘Regular’ in the titles; When the page background changes to a darker color the font color changes from black to color pink and blue to white; there may be meta-text stories aside to distract the reader. The advertising will be up to 30% of the total number of pages of the magazine; the ad support is based appendages on the covers and in the middle, as in scientific journals. The connection between the magazine and the website will be using codes from magazine and recognized on the website as the possibility of acquiring the magazine (digital version) at a more affordable cost of a ‘like’ on facebook.
